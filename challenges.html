Challenges

The objective of Steminority is to recognize the bright minds of the minority youths. Our global challenge is a competition for minority high schoolers across all school boards and put their skilled brains to the test. We pride in awarding students who excel in technology and award them for their accomplishments.

How it Works:

Students will first explore the different challenges that are available under each category of STEM (e.g, Engineering will have multiple different challenges to choose from, such as hands-on robotics construction, or the architectural components of expanding a modern building, etc.) Once a challenge sparks a participant's interest, they will select it and be enrolled to participate on the competition day against other high-schoolers in the same challenge!

Why Join?

Once enrolled into the program and challenge of choice, the levels of advancement from competing are as follows:

Categories:

- City
- Provincial
- National
- International

Prizes are awarded for City, Provincial, National, and a scholarship will be awarded to the lucky winners in International! Even if you don't win, it is a very unique and challening experience to get an understanding of what field of technology you are interested in, possibly opening up a pathway to a future career!
