Who are we?

We are a student-based organization, aiming to be implemented into school districts all around the globe, starting from North America.
Steminority is a platform directed towards minority groups of high-school students, to give a chance at becoming involved in technology and STEM from a younger age through a national hackathon-based competition against others, using various online STEM challenges offered by us FREE of cost!
We will also host mini global challenges to encourage exploration of STEM.
