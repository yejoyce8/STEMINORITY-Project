S - Science

Science, as the study of the natural world and how it works, and is heavily related to technology. We use technology to put scientific knowledge into action in solving problems, and innovating new ideas. Technology and science work hand in hand with each other, as science allows us to build, and technology allows science to progress.

T - Technology

Technology allows us to use our wide range of techniques and skills in producing innovative goods and services for our convenience and societal advancements. The many sectors of technology include but are not limited to, mechanical, electronic, industrial, medical, communications, manufacturing etc.

Engineering permits principles of math and science into play, in developing useful and economical applications in our lives. It is a field of finding out how things work, and solving problems, in making practical uses for scientific discoveries.

M - Mathematics

Mathematics is the overall study of numbers, which include complex topics of quantity, structure, space, and change. It allows our lives and society to function in an orderly fashion, and can allow for the advancement of technology and science. Math is cultivated by critical thinking, problem solving, creativity, reasoning, logic, and many other skills that are necessary in one's lives.
